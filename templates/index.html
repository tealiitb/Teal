About Us

Teal Research Lab is a cutting-edge research facility located at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

The Thermal Remote Sensing and Environmental Applications Lab (TEAL) at the Department of Civil Engineering, IIT Bombay, is dedicated to advancing remote sensing technologies, primarily using thermal remote sensing data for hydrology, water resources, and environmental applications.

Our research focuses on:

  • Evapotranspiration modeling
  • Drought monitoring
  • Soil moisture estimation
  • Land surface temperature modeling and downscaling
  • UAV-based applications in agriculture and water management
  • Gross Primary Production (GPP) estimation
